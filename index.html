About Us

People are always asking us, "but why salmon, though?" and we like to tell them we do it because people like salmon and people also like cookies. That means people should doubly enjoy the great taste of salmon in cookies right? We like to think so, and that's exactly why we're bringing our 4th generation family recipe to all of Seattle. Thank you for your patronage and we hope you enjoy these tasty treats!
